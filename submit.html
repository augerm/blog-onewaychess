About One Way Chess

Our mission is to build the chess community through entertainment and instruction.

© 2016 One Way Chess Inc.

Our Teachers

We take great pride in offering quality chess instruction in our schools. For that reason we meet weekly with our teachers, regularly review our lesson plans and relentlessly innovate. All of our teachers also attend a mandatory orientation session to develop their class room management skills and set them on the right path towards success as an instructor. You may expect nothing but the best from your One Way Chess instructors.

Our Lesson Plans

Our lesson plans were developed personally by the founders of One Way Chess. The combined efforts of a veteran school teacher and a National Master chess player are sure to take your school's after school chess club to the next level! And most of all it is designed for them to have a lot of fun while doing it.

Our Technology

As a company that loves technology we couldn't help but incorporate our tech into the classroom. One Way Chess is re-inventing the way chess instruction works. Through our software 'Chess Tech' we will deliver data driven lesson plans and testing capabilities for teachers to engage their students outside of the classroom. While this technology is still in beta we expect it to be coming to a classroom near you very soon!

Our Imagination

At One Way Chess we have a different philosophy than the rest of the world when it comes to chess. We believe chess should be exciting. We are disruptively innovating the world of chess with our project the National Chess League and we expect to deliver the same results to our schools. Our mission is to continue to grow the chess world and unlock the many benefits that chess offers to it's constituents.

Our Team

We're dedicated to your school's success. It's our mission to continue to knock down boundaries and expand the chess world. In order to do that we believe we need to start where it matters the most in the school system. Therefore, the school system is our top priority and as our client you will have our full attention at all times.

Our Experience

Between our two founders we have collectively been in over 25 schools in our time instructing chess. We've seen every angle that a school can throw at us and we're ready for it. So bring it all on and we look forward to showing you why One Way Chess is the last chess instruction company that you will ever need.
